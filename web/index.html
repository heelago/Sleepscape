the drawing

Every stroke is
always beautiful

Every stroke is mirrored across multiple axes simultaneously. Whatever you draw becomes a perfectly symmetrical pattern. The app does the geometry — you just move your finger.

Three modes: mandala symmetry in 4, 6, 8, 12, or 16-fold, ellipse mode for concentric shapes, and free drawing with smooth glowing strokes. Ripple rings bloom from every touch.

❋ mandala ◯ ellipse ✦ free 4 · 6 · 8 · 12 · 16 fold ripple rings pressure sensitive apple pencil

the sound

Music that
never repeats

The audio is generated in real time using AudioKit — not a loop, not a recording. Seven notes from the A pentatonic scale, each one breathing in and out on its own independent cycle. They overlap, drift apart, layer up, fall silent, and return.

Each note plays slightly differently in your left and right ear, creating a binaural beating effect in the 3–4 Hz range — the delta wave frequency your brain produces during deep sleep. Use headphones for the full effect.

Soft piano-key tones surface every few seconds and decay slowly. A wandering melody voice glides between notes with long, slow portamento. Three frequency modes: delta for sleep, theta for meditation, 528 Hz for an open, warm feeling.

generative · never loops binaural beats delta · theta · 528hz piano bells AudioKit plays in background

the breathing guide

It appears when
you go still

Stop drawing for a few seconds and a breathing ring appears at the centre of the canvas — expanding on the inhale, contracting on the exhale. It disappears the moment you start drawing again.

Choose from clinically-supported patterns: 4-7-8 for sleep onset, box breathing for grounding, cardiac coherence for anxiety, resonance breathing for calm. Or set your own timing exactly.

4-7-8 box breathing cardiac coherence resonance · 6-6 custom timing
